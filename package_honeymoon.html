Deposit Policy
A one night deposit will be required at the time of making your reservation, with the remaining balance due payable upon arrival to the retreat. All reservations made in high season period will also be secured with a one night deposit, however balance for accommodation will be due 14 days prior to arrival. Once final payment of your booking has been made, confirmation of that payment and your booking will be sent to you via either email or post to the address you provided at the time you made this reservation. If any deposit or final payment is not received by the due date, Santai reserves the right to cancel the booking. Any monies paid by you in relation to your booking will be forfeited to Santai Retreat.
Payment Methods
To check the validity of your credit card and guarantee your reservation, the retreat reserves the right to secure the room and to cover an incidentals. If paying by cash, a valid copy of identification will be requested, along with a $200 cash bond that is refundable upon your departure. We do not accept Amex or Diners. Using Visa or Mastercard will attract a 1.5% surcharge which will be applied your statement. Should you wish to pre-pay your balance by electronic transfer, funds must be cleared into the retreat’s bank account prior to your arrival. Please contact Guest Services for these details if required.
